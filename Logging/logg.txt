Logging Categores and levels

1. Debug : detailed information typically of interest only when diagnosing a problem
2. info : confirms that things are working as expected. 
3. Warning : An indication that something unexpected has happened or will happen.
4. Error : Unable to perform the operations
5. Critical : A serious error, indicating that the program itself may be unable to continue running


default logging levels are warning and higher i.e. error and critical
